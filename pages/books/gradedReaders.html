Graded Readers

Graded readers are specialized resources crafted for language learners to facilitate gradual progression in reading proficiency. Tailored to different levels of language proficiency, these readers offer texts with simplified language and structures, ensuring accessibility and comprehension for learners at varying stages. They encompass a diverse range of topics and genres, providing engaging content while reinforcing language skills.

Please keep in mind that some content may contain mature topics and language.
